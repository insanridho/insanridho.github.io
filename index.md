I am a transport and energy transition professional based in Jakarta, Indonesia. My work focuses on **zero-emission vehicles (ZEV)**, electric mobility policy, and transport decarbonisation across Indonesia and Southeast Asia.

Currently a **Transport Specialist at WRI Indonesia**, I lead initiatives at the intersection of clean energy policy and urban mobility — working with government agencies, development partners, and industry to accelerate Indonesia's transition to low-carbon transport.

My background spans over a decade across policy, program management, engineering, and operations — giving me the ability to engage credibly from technical detail through to ministerial-level stakeholder dialogue.
