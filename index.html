About me

I am a transport and energy transition professional based in Jakarta, Indonesia. My work focuses on zero-emission vehicles (ZEV), electric mobility policy, and transport decarbonisation across Indonesia and Southeast Asia.

I am currently a Transport Specialist at WRI Indonesia, leading policy initiatives on clean energy and urban mobility. I work with government agencies, development partners, and industry to support Indonesia's transition to low-carbon transport.

My background covers over a decade across policy, program management, engineering, and operations. This range of experience allows me to engage across technical, operational, and ministerial levels.

I am open to collaboration on ZEV policy, electric mobility research, and transport decarbonisation. Connect with me via the contact page.
