Selected publications, policy reports, and op-eds on transport decarbonisation, zero-emission vehicles, and urban mobility in Indonesia.

---

## Policy Reports & Technical Papers

* **CTCN UNIDO Closure Report: Supporting Jakarta's Transition to E-Mobility** (June 2021)
  Contributing author. Report covers electric bus business models, financing analysis, and policy recommendations for Jakarta's transition to electric public transport.

* **"Measuring Staff Availability in Public Transport"** — *IOP Conference Series: Earth and Environmental Science* (August 2020)
  Peer-reviewed paper based on MSc thesis research on staff scheduling and availability modelling in the Tyne and Wear Metro system.

## Op-Eds & Commentary

* **"Menganulir Mobil Listrik sebagai Jawaban Krisis BBM"** — *Kumparan* (March 2022)
  Opinion piece examining the role of electric vehicles in Indonesia's fuel supply crisis debate.

* **"Perpanjangan Diskon PPnBM dan Nasib Transportasi Publik"** — *Detik* (September 2021)
  Commentary on the extension of Indonesia's luxury goods sales tax (PPnBM) discount and its implications for public transport investment.

* **"Musnahnya Perkembangan Bahan Bakar Gas di Indonesia"** — *Geotimes* (October 2021)
  Analysis of the decline of natural gas vehicle development in Indonesia and lessons for the EV transition.
